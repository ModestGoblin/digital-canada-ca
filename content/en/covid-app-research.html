Do you want to help stop the spread of COVID-19?

The Canadian Digital Service (CDS) is working to help the government’s response to COVID-19.

We are looking to gather feedback on an app we are building to slow the spread of COVID-19. If you live in Canada, are over the age of 18, and would like to participate in this research, please continue reading.

The study will be conducted through an online video-conferencing session and should take about 30 minutes to 1 hour.

No expertise about COVID-19 is required.

How to get involved

If you are interested in volunteering to participate in a study:

  1. Contact cds-snc@tbs-sct.gc.ca to express your interest
  2. We will then ask you some preliminary questions to help us determine how you may qualify for our studies
  3. We may then contact you to participate in a study

By participating in this study, you understand that:

  • You are volunteering to participate. You can stop at any time for any reason.
  • Your information will be confidential. This means your responses will not be linked to you.
  • Your participation and answers will not affect your access to Government of Canada services or benefits.
  • CDS may collect your personal information. For example, your age, province, experience using government services, experience using the application being developed, general experience during the COVID-19 pandemic. To learn about how CDS protects your personal information, please see the attached privacy statement.

Privacy Notice

Participation is completely voluntary.

For any questions about this research, please contact:

cds-snc@tbs-sct.gc.ca

Thank you for volunteering to talk to us.

What we will collect

By participating in this research you consent that your age, gender, province, experience using government services, experience using the application being developed, general experience during the COVID-19 pandemic will be collected.

How we will use this information

The Canadian Digital Service (CDS) will use this information to gather feedback and improve an application being built in an effort to slow the spread of COVID-19 across Canada.

We collect this information to ensure our research cohorts are diverse and to identify trends in feedback for specific groups.

This personal information will not be used for any administrative purposes. Your information will not be used to make a decision that affects you or your access to Government of Canada services.

If you have consented to being recorded, the recording may be disclosed to other departments to show them real feedback on the prototype.

CDS will own the usability testing information and may publish or share with other government institutions a summary of what we have learned from this research, including quotes or narratives. Your name will not be associated with your responses, quotes, or narratives.

Who we are

The Canadian Digital Service is part of the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat (TBS).

The collection and use of your personal information by TBS is authorized by the Financial Administration Act.

The collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information is in accordance with the federal Privacy Act. Under the Privacy Act, you have a right to protection of, access to and correction or notation of your personal information.

The Standard Personal Information Bank entitled “Outreach Activities, PSU 938” describes the personal information that may be written down.
