Do you want to help stop the spread of COVID-19?

The Canadian Digital Service (CDS) is building an Exposure Notification app to help stop the spread of COVID-19 in Canada.

This app is now in its Beta phase and we need your help to test it.

Beta phase means the software is built, but we need people (like you!) to test the app and ensure itâ€™s working smoothly.

If you live in Canada, are over the age of 18, and would like to participate in this research, please continue reading.

No expertise about COVID-19 is needed.

What you can do to help:

  1. Sign up here.
  2. Download the app to your phone (we will follow up with instructions on how to do this).
  3. Complete a task using the app.
  4. Provide feedback on your experience.

The entire process will take 2 - 4 days.

By participating in this Beta study, you understand that:

  • You are volunteering to participate. You can stop at any time for any reason.
  • Your information will be confidential. This means your responses will not be linked to you.
  • Your participation and answers will not affect your access to Government of Canada services or benefits.

To learn about how the Canadian Digital Service protects your personal information, please contact us at cds-snc@tbs-sct.gc.ca.
